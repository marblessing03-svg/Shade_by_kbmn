Process

Four steps to your perfect look

01

Grant Camera Access

Allow camera access in your browser. Your camera activates instantly — no download, no app, no sign-up required. Works on any device with a front-facing camera.

02

Real-Time Face Scan

Position your face within the guide in natural light. Our algorithm samples thousands of skin pixels over 3.5 seconds, building a precise spectral profile of your exact complexion.

03

Shade Identification

We identify your shade name, depth level, and precise undertone — warm, cool, or neutral — placing you within our 12-point spectrum from Porcelain to Ebony.

04

11-Product Look Curation

Our AI beauty consultant instantly recommends all eleven makeup essentials — foundation, concealer, blush, bronzer, highlighter, eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara, lip color, setting powder, and contour — from real prestige and drugstore brands, perfectly matched to your shade.
